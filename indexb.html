More About Iceland

Iceland is a stunning country located in the North Atlantic Ocean and is known for its incredible landscapes, including glaciers, waterfalls, hot springs, and geysers. It is a popular travel destination for nature enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and photographers. With its unique culture, friendly locals, and delicious cuisine, Iceland offers a one-of-a-kind travel experience. Whether you want to go hiking, whale watching, or simply admire the northern lights, Iceland is a must-visit destination.

Urridafoss Waterfall

Urridafoss is a waterfall located in the south of Iceland, on the river Þjórsá. It is considered one of the most voluminous waterfall in Iceland. The word "urriða" means trout in Icelandic and this waterfall is a popular spot for fishing, as well as for hiking and sightseeing.
